# Competitive positioning notes

Short differentiation for workshop follow-ups. **Not a benchmark study**—no performance claims without measured data.

## Meta TRIBE v2 (participant suggestion, Cycle 1)

**Context (public positioning):** Meta’s TRIBE research direction explores brain-inspired / efficient AI architectures. TRIBE v2 is discussed in the industry as part of Meta AI’s long-term efficiency and reasoning research—not as a shipped retail banking risk product.

### How AlphaVision differs today

| Dimension | AlphaVision Insights | TRIBE-style research (typical) |
|-----------|----------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Product shape** | Open-source S&P 100 **risk dashboard** for asset managers | Research framework / architecture exploration |
| **Primary user** | Asset managers (research/education) | General AI research community |
| **Outputs** | VaR, sentiment, portfolio views, educational chat | Architecture benchmarks, not VaR compliance packs |
| **Stack** | Classical VaR + DeepAR + FinBERT on standard Python | Neuromorphic / brain-inspired efficiency focus |
| **Regulatory story** | Disclaimers, provenance API, operator privacy checklist | Not positioned as regulated finance tooling |

### Differentiation message (one paragraph)

AlphaVision is a **transparent, classical-first risk helper** with explicit data-as-of provenance and honest labeling of ML vs statistical outputs. TRIBE-class work is complementary **upstream research** on efficient cognition; our near-term value is **explainable risk context for asset managers** on public market data, while neuromorphic pilots remain **Use Case 2** until validated in sandbox evidence.
